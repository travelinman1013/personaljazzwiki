## Biography
Pink Teddy Bear is a musical project based in New Orleans, Louisiana, known for its unique blend of exotica, jungle, and experimental soundscapes. The project is led by Carlos Grasso, who draws inspiration from the flora, fauna, and magical Southern mystique of the Tiki Farm in Pearlington, Mississippi. Pink Teddy Bear’s music is characterized by its use of field recordings, samples, power drills, keyboards, synths, wine glasses, and various percussion and electronic elements, creating a rich, immersive experience that pays homage to the exotica music of the 1950s and 1960s, as well as film soundtracks and sci-fi themes. The debut album, 'Welcome To The Tiki Farm,' showcases this eclectic approach, inviting listeners to explore a world where nature and sound intertwine in unexpected ways. Pink Teddy Bear’s work has been described as a savory music and visual experience, reflecting the vibrant and diverse cultural landscape of New Orleans.
