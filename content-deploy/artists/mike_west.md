## Biography
Mike West is a Ghanaian musician and DJ based in the United Kingdom, recognized for his dynamic presence in the reggae and roots reggae scene. With a career that spans multiple countries, West has established himself not only as a performer but also as a cultural bridge, bringing African and Caribbean musical influences to European audiences. His early years in Ghana provided a foundation rich in traditional rhythms, which he later fused with reggae after relocating to the UK.

Throughout his career, West has worked extensively as a DJ in France, Switzerland, and Austria, further broadening his musical palette and audience. His performances are known for their energetic delivery and authentic reggae sound, often incorporating elements from his Ghanaian heritage. West’s ability to blend genres and cultures has earned him a reputation as a versatile and innovative artist within the European reggae community.

Mike West’s legacy lies in his role as a musical ambassador, introducing reggae and African-inspired sounds to new listeners across Europe. His work continues to inspire both audiences and aspiring musicians, highlighting the global reach and unifying power of reggae music.
