## Biography
Willie Lane is a guitarist from New England, known for his work in the psych scene. His early career involved collaborations with experimental units, and he was based in Philadelphia before moving to Western Massachusetts. Lane's music often blends elements of American Primitive and psychedelic styles, creating a unique sound that is both abstract and melodic. His albums, such as 'Known Quantity' and 'Guitar Army of One,' showcase his ability to layer complex sounds, often giving the impression of playing duets with himself. Lane has been associated with the New England psych scene, working alongside artists like MV & EE and Stella Kola.
