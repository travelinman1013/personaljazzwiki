## Biography
Epi Martinez is a respected figure in the Tejano and Conjunto music scene, known for his work as the leader of Epi Martinez & Friends. Originating from South Texas, Martinez has dedicated his career to preserving and innovating within the traditional sounds of Conjunto, a genre rooted in the cultural heritage of the Texas-Mexico borderlands. His early life in this musically rich region exposed him to the accordion-driven rhythms and storytelling that define the genre, inspiring him to pursue music from a young age.

Throughout his career, Epi Martinez has become recognized for his ability to blend classic Conjunto elements with contemporary influences, making the music accessible to new generations while honoring its roots. His group, Epi Martinez & Friends, is celebrated for lively performances at festivals such as the Narciso Martinez Conjunto Festival, where they bring energy and authenticity to the stage. Martinez's recent projects include creative reinterpretations of iconic songs, such as adding a Conjunto flavor to the country classic 'Luckenbach, Texas,' showcasing his commitment to cross-genre collaboration and cultural fusion.

Epi Martinez's legacy is marked by his contributions to the preservation of Conjunto music and his role in fostering community through performance and collaboration. His work continues to earn accolades from organizations like the South Texas Conjunto Association, solidifying his place as a key figure in the ongoing story of Tejano and Conjunto music.
