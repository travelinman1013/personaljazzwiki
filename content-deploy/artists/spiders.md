## Biography
Spiders is a Swedish rock band formed in Gothenburg, known for their blend of stoner rock and psychedelic rock, drawing strong inspiration from the sounds of the 1970s. The band was founded by Ann-Sofie Hoyles and John Hoyles, who have been the core creative force behind Spiders since their inception. Their music is characterized by heavy guitar riffs, vintage tones, and a retro aesthetic that pays homage to classic rock and stoner rock traditions. Spiders have released several critically acclaimed albums, including 'Flash Point' (2012), 'Shake Electric' (2014), 'Killer Machine' (2018), and 'Sharp Objects' (2025), each showcasing their evolving sound and commitment to the genre. Over the years, Spiders have built a reputation for their energetic live performances and have become a staple in the European stoner rock scene, earning a dedicated fanbase and respect from critics for their authentic approach to the genre.
