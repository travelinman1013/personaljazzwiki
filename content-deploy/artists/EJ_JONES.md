## Biography
EJ Jones, also known as 'The Chosen Child,' is a rising American soul singer from Memphis, Tennessee. He first gained attention in mid-2024 by posting phone-recorded covers of classic soul songs on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. By January 2025, he began uploading content consistently, quickly amassing a significant following for his heartfelt renditions and smooth vocal style. His breakthrough came with the release of his first original single, 'Too Good,' in March 2025, followed by 'Gas Station Love,' both of which showcased his ability to blend classic soul influences with a contemporary sensibility.[1][2][3]

Jones's musical style is deeply rooted in the traditions of soul, drawing inspiration from legendary artists like Sam Cooke, Bobby Womack, and Johnnie Taylor. His covers, including a widely praised rendition of 'A Change Is Gonna Come,' have been recognized by the official Sam Cooke Facebook page, further cementing his reputation as an 'old soul' in the modern era. Industry observers have noted his ability to channel the emotional depth and authenticity of his influences while bringing a fresh perspective to the genre.[1][2][3]

As his popularity continues to grow, EJ Jones is celebrated for revitalizing classic soul for a new generation. His online presence, particularly on TikTok and Instagram, has helped him reach a broad audience, and his consistent output of both covers and original material suggests a promising future in the music industry.[1][2][3]
