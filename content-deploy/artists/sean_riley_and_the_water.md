## Biography
Sean Riley is a New Orleans-based singer, songwriter, and guitarist known for his roots-heavy sound and dynamic performances with his backing band, The Water. Born and raised in the North Country of New York, Riley honed his musical craft in the vibrant music scene of New York City before eventually settling in New Orleans, where he found his artistic home. His music draws deeply from the traditions of Southern folk, blues, and roots music, blending heartfelt songwriting with a raw, authentic delivery that pays tribute to the rich cultural heritage of New Orleans. Sean Riley & The Water have become a familiar presence in the city’s music scene, performing regularly in venues from the Bywater to the French Quarter and earning recognition for their flowing, collaborative style.

The name 'The Water' reflects the fluidity of the band’s lineup, with different musicians frequently joining Riley on stage, creating a constantly evolving musical experience. Riley’s debut album, 'Stone Cold Hands,' released on Pugnacious Records, showcases his talents as a vocalist, guitarist, and dobro player, and stands as a musical tribute to the traditions of New Orleans and Southern folk. His work has been praised for its emotional depth and connection to the region’s musical legacy, establishing him as a distinctive voice in the contemporary roots music landscape.
