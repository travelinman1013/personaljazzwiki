## Biography
Breeze Mr. Do 2 Much is a Southern Soul artist known for his heartfelt lyrics, self-made artistry, and strong Mississippi roots. Born in Jackson, Mississippi, due to the absence of a maternity ward in nearby Philadelphia, he was raised in Philadelphia and currently resides in Meridian, a city with a rich musical heritage. Breeze began his musical journey in college, but it was a Southern Soul radio broadcast in 2021 that inspired him to shift from R&B to Southern Soul, reigniting his passion for music.

In 2022, Breeze co-founded the group Jae and Breeze, releasing their debut album 'Down in the Country,' which quickly gained traction online. Shortly after, he launched a solo career, achieving a breakthrough with the track 'Soul Party.' His signature hit, 'Love Me For Real,' released in May 2025 and produced by Flowbeatz, has garnered over 400,000 streams and climbed the Southern Soul charts, earning praise for its authenticity and relatable themes. Breeze is recognized for his one-man-band approach, handling writing, singing, recording, and production himself, and his music resonates for its honesty and timeless Southern Soul feel.

Breeze's career has included collaborations with Southern Soul icons and performances at major events like the Jackson Music Awards. His style blends classic Southern Soul with modern sensibilities, marked by emotional storytelling and a deep connection to his Mississippi heritage. As his influence grows, Breeze Mr. Do 2 Much is establishing himself as a significant new voice in contemporary Southern Soul.
