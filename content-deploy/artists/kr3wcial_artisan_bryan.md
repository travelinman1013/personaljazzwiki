## Biography
Kr3wcial is a hip hop artist, producer, and sound engineer hailing from Algiers, a neighborhood in New Orleans, Louisiana. Raised in a musically rich environment, his earliest memories include watching his father, Tony Gullage, rehearse with legendary New Orleans jazz musicians such as Irma Thomas, Davell Crawford, and Henry Butler. This upbringing immersed him in the city's vibrant musical traditions, shaping his creative outlook from a young age. Kr3wcial began writing raps in middle school and quickly progressed to recording and producing music by his late teens, often working with friends and local artists.

As his career developed, Kr3wcial became known for his introspective lyrics and commitment to spreading messages of truth, love, and resilience. He is a core member of the New Orleans-based collective glbl wrmng, performing at major events like the BUKU festival and the New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival. His work blends classic hip hop with the soulful, improvisational spirit of New Orleans, and he is recognized for his versatility as both a performer and producer. Kr3wcial's legacy is rooted in his dedication to artistic growth, community collaboration, and using music as a tool for positive change.
