## Biography
Famous Pluto (Osahon Grandeur Uzama, born August 14, 2006) and Zerrydl (Divine Uzama, born December 4, 2002) are Nigerian brothers from Benin City, Edo State, known for their contributions to afrobeats, amapiano, and afropluto styles. Raised alongside their older brother Shallipopi in a musically inclined family, they began sharing freestyles and covers on social media in 2015. Zerrydl, the eldest of the two, started rapping consistently from 2021, releasing his debut single 'Ifeoma' in 2022 and breaking through with 'Puff & Pass' in 2023, which charted on TurnTable Top 100. Famous Pluto was initially reluctant but was encouraged by Shallipopi, drawing inspiration from Wizkid; he gained traction via a viral TikTok feature on 'Feel D Mood' in 2024.[1][2][5]
