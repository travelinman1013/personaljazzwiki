## Fun Facts
- Jelly Roll’s stage name was given to him by his mother because he was a 'chubby kid.'
- He wrote his first rap song at age 9 or 10, inspired by wanting to make music that moved people like his mother’s favorite songs did.
- Jelly Roll sold his earliest mixtapes in high school parking lots and even included them with drug sales during his troubled youth.
- His first performance at the Grand Ole Opry was a personal milestone, fulfilling a lifelong dream after years of struggle.
