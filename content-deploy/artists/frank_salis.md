## Biography
Frank Salis is a Swiss pianist, guitarist, organist, and Hammond organ specialist known for his work in jazz, soul, funk, blues, and New Orleans-influenced styles. A musical prodigy, he received a piano and instruction book at age 8, sparking his early development as a multi-instrumentalist. Hailing from Switzerland, Salis has built an international career blending European jazz sensibilities with American grooves, performing at global festivals and collaborating across scenes.[2][3][4]
