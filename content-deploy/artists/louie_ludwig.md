## Biography
Louie Ludwig is a New Orleans-based singer-songwriter known for his eclectic and adventurous approach to American roots music. Active on the NOLA scene for years, Ludwig has built a reputation as a musical bon vivant and self-described 'unreconstructed hippie,' with a style that blends folk, rock, and experimental elements. His work often features storytelling lyrics and a willingness to explore diverse sonic textures, resulting in music that is both grounded in tradition and unafraid to take risks.[3][4]

Ludwig's career has included collaborations with a variety of musicians, most notably as the frontman of Louie Ludwig with The Moss Pickers, whose album 'Elevation 13' showcases his genre-blurring tendencies. His 2017 release 'I Got Nothin To Say' further highlights his ability to assemble talented friends and musicians to create an 'electric variety of sounds,' reflecting the vibrant and collaborative spirit of the New Orleans music community.[3][4]

While Ludwig's popularity remains largely regional, his contributions to the local scene have made him a respected figure among peers and fans. His music, marked by both wit and introspection, continues to resonate with listeners who appreciate authenticity and creative freedom.
