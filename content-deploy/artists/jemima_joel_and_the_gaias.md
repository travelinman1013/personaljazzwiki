## Biography
Jemima Joél is a New Orleans-based singer, painter, and performer known as 'The Woman Who Sings and Paints,' who leads the band Jemima Joél and the Sounds of Gaia. She created her personal brand to intertwine her musical and visual art talents, drawing inspiration from Gaia, the ancestral mother of earth and life, which informs the band's thematic essence. As the lead singer and painter, Joél has been building her presence in the local music scene, including appearances like a Juneteenth interview discussing her band's origins and upcoming artworks.[1][2][3]
