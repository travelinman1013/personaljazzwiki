## Biography
Conjunto Massalia is a musical ensemble based in Marseille, France, specializing in **cha cha cha** and **son cubano**, genres rooted in the Cuban musical tradition blending African and European influences. Formed in the French port city known for its multicultural vibrancy, the group emerged within the contemporary world music scene, focusing on retro-flavored Latin dance styles such as rumba, mambo, and cha cha chá. Their music revives these timeless Cuban rhythms for modern audiences, as evidenced by their contributions to international compilations.[1][2]
