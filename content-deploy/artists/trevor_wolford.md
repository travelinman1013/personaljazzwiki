## Biography
Trevor Wolford is an American guitarist, composer, and producer known for his work in jazz, gospel, and contemporary music. He began his musical journey at the remarkably young age of six, performing in church and sitting in with jazz musicians in Atlanta, Georgia. This early exposure to both sacred and improvisational music laid the foundation for a career marked by versatility and technical prowess. Over the years, Wolford has developed a reputation as a skilled collaborator, working with a range of artists across genres, and contributing to both live performances and studio recordings. His style blends elements of jazz fusion, gospel, and modern production, reflecting his diverse musical upbringing and ongoing creative evolution.
