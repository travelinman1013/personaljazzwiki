## Biography
Biga*Ranx, whose real name is Gabriel Piotrowski, is a French ragga hip hop musician and producer from Tours in the Centre Region of France. Born on September 27, he has established himself as a pioneering figure in the contemporary reggae and electronic music landscape. As the head of the "Vapor Dub" movement—a distinctive fusion of dub and cloudy electro aesthetics—Biga*Ranx has carved out a unique sonic identity that blends traditional reggae elements with modern electronic production techniques. Signed to X-Ray Production, he has become known for his innovative approach to reggae music, incorporating atmospheric production and experimental soundscapes into his work. His career has been marked by consistent artistic evolution and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of reggae and hip hop fusion.
