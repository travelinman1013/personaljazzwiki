## Biography
Jenn Howard is a soulful R&B and blues singer-songwriter born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana. Deeply influenced by the city’s rich musical heritage, Howard developed her powerful, emotive vocal style from an early age, performing in local venues and absorbing the traditions of jazz, blues, and gospel that define New Orleans’ sound. Her upbringing in this vibrant cultural environment laid the foundation for a career marked by authenticity and heartfelt expression.[1]

Howard’s career blossomed as she became known for her mesmerizing live performances and original songwriting. She has released acclaimed work, including the album 'Valiant Women,' which she describes as a 'real labor of love.' This record showcases her ability to blend classic blues and R&B with contemporary sensibilities, earning praise for its warmth and depth.[5] Howard’s shows often feature a mix of her own compositions and tributes to legendary artists, reflecting both her reverence for tradition and her unique artistic voice.[6]
