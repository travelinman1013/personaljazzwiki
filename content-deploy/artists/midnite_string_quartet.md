## Biography
Midnite String Quartet is an American classical and classical crossover ensemble renowned for their innovative string arrangements of popular music. Emerging as a leader in the genre, the group has captivated audiences with lush, expressive renditions of contemporary hits, seamlessly blending classical technique with modern sensibilities. Their music is characterized by a rich, melodic sound that is both entrancing and calming, appealing to fans of both classical and pop music.

The quartet has built a substantial discography, releasing albums that reinterpret the works of artists such as the Jonas Brothers, Shakira, and Dua Lipa. Their arrangements have garnered significant attention, notably with their version of 'Wrecking Ball,' which was featured as the climactic moment in Season 2 of the Netflix series Bridgerton. Midnite String Quartet's dedication to their craft and their ability to bridge genres have earned them a devoted following and a respected place in the world of classical crossover music. Their ongoing output includes themed albums and singles that showcase their versatility and technical mastery, ensuring their continued influence and popularity.
