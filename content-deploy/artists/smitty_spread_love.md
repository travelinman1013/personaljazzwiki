## Biography
Renard Smith Sr., known as Smitty Spread Love, is a rapper originally from New Orleans, Louisiana, now based in Houston, Texas. He gained local attention as a rideshare driver for Uber and Lyft, where he performs original music for passengers to spread positivity and relieve their stress. His music emphasizes themes of love, unity, and uniqueness, as heard in lyrics like 'Spread Love' and 'If we all a little different that makes us more complete.'[2][3]

Smitty Spread Love developed his career uniquely by integrating rapping into his daily rideshare work, turning ordinary trips into performances that uplift riders. Featured on Houston Life in 2019, he shared his goal of making people feel better through his music, rapping live during interviews and handing out holiday cheer with custom Christmas raps and gift cards in later appearances. His style stands out in a crowded music scene with a positive, motivational message, earning him nicknames like 'Unicorn Blessings' from fans.[2][3][6]

While his Spotify popularity remains low at 2 with unknown genres, Smitty continues building a grassroots following through personal interactions and collaborations like tracks with John Fuggin Dough. His legacy lies in authentically spreading love via music in everyday settings, inspiring riders and local media without major label backing.[2][7]
