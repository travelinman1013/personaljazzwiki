## Biography
Andrew Molina is a renowned ukulele virtuoso based in Maui, Hawaii. He began playing the ukulele at the age of 13 and has since become one of the most celebrated ukulele artists globally. His career has been marked by a unique fusion of genres and innovative use of effects with the ukulele. Molina has released three albums: 'The Beginning', 'A New Journey', and 'evolve', all of which have been nominated for 'Ukulele Album of the Year' at the prestigious Nā Hōkū Hanohano Awards. He has toured extensively across the U.S., Europe, China, and Tahiti, captivating audiences with his electrifying performances. Molina's music has also been featured in various media, including video promotions for Hawaiian Airlines and OceanVodka. He continues to inspire new generations of musicians through his Andrew Molina Ukulele Academy, an online instructional course.

Andrew Molina's musical style is influenced by a range of artists, including Jake Shimabukuro, Sting, Rodrigo Y Gabriela, and John Mayer. His performances often blend traditional ukulele techniques with modern effects, creating a distinctive sound that has captivated audiences worldwide. Molina's legacy extends beyond his own music, as he continues to push the boundaries of what is possible on the ukulele, inspiring both aspiring musicians and seasoned professionals alike.

Through his work, Andrew Molina has established himself as a leading figure in the ukulele community, known for his technical mastery and innovative approach to the instrument. His contributions to the world of music have been recognized by the Hawai`i Academy of Recording Arts, among other prestigious organizations.
