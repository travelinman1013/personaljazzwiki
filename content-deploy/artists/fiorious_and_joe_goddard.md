## Biography
Fiorious & Joe Goddard is a collaborative project between Bronx-born singer-songwriter Fiorious and British musician, producer, and DJ Joe Goddard, best known as a founding member of Hot Chip. Fiorious, celebrated for his soulful vocals and energetic presence in the disco house and nu disco scenes, has steadily built a reputation for blending classic house influences with modern pop and funk. Joe Goddard, hailing from London, has been a pivotal figure in electronic music since the early 2000s, co-founding the influential band Hot Chip and the Greco-Roman label, and releasing acclaimed solo and group works.

Their partnership emerged from a mutual admiration for dance music’s rich history and a shared commitment to pushing the boundaries of contemporary club sounds. Notable collaborations include the singles “Do It All Again” and “New World (Flow),” which showcase Fiorious’s dynamic vocals over Goddard’s lush, synth-driven production. Their music draws on the traditions of disco, house, and funk, infusing them with a modern, euphoric sensibility that resonates on dancefloors and beyond. The duo’s work is characterized by uplifting melodies, intricate rhythms, and a celebration of inclusivity and joy in club culture.

While both artists maintain vibrant solo careers, their joint output has been praised for its ability to bridge generations of dance music lovers and for its authentic homage to the roots of house and disco. Their collaborations are seen as a testament to the enduring power of creative synergy across borders and backgrounds.
