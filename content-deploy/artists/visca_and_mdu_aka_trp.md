## Biography
Visca & MDU aka TRP are influential figures in the South African Amapiano scene, a genre that blends deep house, jazz, and kwaito influences. MDU aka TRP, born Mduduzi Mangena in Diepkloof, Johannesburg, South Africa, is widely credited as a pioneer of the log drum sound, a defining feature of Amapiano music. His innovative production style and futuristic approach have helped shape the genre's sonic identity, making him one of its most respected producers and DJs[1][2][3][4].

Visca, also a visionary Amapiano producer, has gained recognition for his creative contributions and collaborations within the genre. Both artists began their musical journeys at a young age, with MDU aka TRP showing an early passion for music and actively participating in various musical activities before rising to prominence[4][5]. Their partnership and individual careers have been marked by a commitment to pushing the boundaries of Amapiano, earning them a loyal following and significant influence on the genre's evolution.

Through frequent collaborations and a distinctive musical style characterized by percussive basslines, synth-driven melodies, and rhythmic complexity, Visca & MDU aka TRP have left a lasting legacy in South African music. Their work continues to inspire emerging artists and shape the future of Amapiano, both locally and internationally.
