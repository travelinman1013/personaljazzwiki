## Biography
Robert Glasper, Terrace Martin, and 9th Wonder are renowned musicians known for their contributions to jazz, hip-hop, and neo-soul. Robert Glasper, a pianist and producer, has bridged jazz and hip-hop throughout his career, notably with his album *Black Radio*. He met Terrace Martin at a jazz camp in Denver when they were teenagers. Terrace Martin is a versatile saxophonist and producer, celebrated for his work with Kendrick Lamar and other notable artists. 9th Wonder, a producer and educator, has collaborated with numerous hip-hop artists and teaches hip-hop history at prestigious universities. Together, they formed the supergroup Dinner Party, blending their styles to create a unique sound that reflects their shared history and musical influences. Their music often addresses social issues, such as police brutality, in a calm yet impactful manner. The group's debut EP was recorded in 2019 and features tracks like 'Freeze Tag,' which reflects on police violence. Through their collaborations, they have influenced a new generation of musicians by showcasing the intersection of jazz and hip-hop as powerful tools for social commentary and artistic expression.
