# Erik Jekabson

## Biography
Erik Jekabson is a freelance trumpet player, composer, arranger, and educator based in the San Francisco Bay Area. He maintains an active career performing throughout the region as a bandleader with various groups while also composing and teaching.[1][2][3][4][5][6][7][8] His professional engagements include touring with jazz legends Illinois Jacquet and John Santos, highlighting his roots in traditional and contemporary jazz scenes.[1]

Jekabson's musical style centers on jazz, where he balances performance, composition, and education equally. He leads ensembles like the Erik Jekabson Sextet and Quintet, performing original works in Bay Area venues such as The Sound Room.[3][8] As an educator, he contributes to institutions like the Jazzschool, fostering the next generation of musicians.[5]

While specific details on his early life remain limited in available sources, Jekabson's enduring presence in the Bay Area jazz community underscores his legacy as a versatile contributor to the genre, with a focus on freelance work and local performances.[1][2][4]
