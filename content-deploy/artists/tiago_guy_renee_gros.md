## Biography
Tiago Guy is a Brazilian-born singer-songwriter who relocated to New Orleans in spring 2021 to pursue his passion for American roots music. Since his arrival, he has established himself within the local music scene, performing at prominent venues including Tipitina's, the Maple Leaf Bar, and the New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival. His musical approach blends smooth, soulful sounds inspired by his experiences in New Orleans with introspective lyrics that document a foreigner's perspective on quintessential city experiences, from weathering hurricanes to navigating relationships and family dynamics in the greater New Orleans area.

Renée Gros is a New Orleans-born singer, songwriter, and emerging artist known for her powerful, soulful voice. Based in her native New Orleans, she was drawn to music from an early age and has developed into a recognized figure in the local music scene. Gros frequently collaborates with her husband, Tiago Guy, and together they represent a dynamic musical partnership within the New Orleans artistic community.

As a collaborative unit, Tiago Guy and Renée Gros have performed together at major festivals and venues, including their joint appearance at the New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival in 2024. Their partnership combines Tiago's Brazilian-influenced songwriting sensibilities with Renée's powerful vocal presence, creating a distinctive sound rooted in American roots music traditions while incorporating diverse musical influences.
