## Biography
Junko Beat is a New Orleans-based band founded and led by drummer and keyboardist Chris Lacinak. The project emerged in 2016, after Lacinak decided to step away from extensive touring to focus on developing a new, genre-blending sound. Over two years, Junko Beat crafted a unique musical identity, fusing elements of cosmic funk, electronica, and jam band improvisation. Their debut release, 'Satirifunk,' showcases seven original compositions that defy conventional genre boundaries, highlighting the band's experimental approach and Lacinak's compositional prowess.

The band's music is characterized by intricate rhythms, layered keyboard textures, and a playful yet sophisticated approach to funk and electronic music. Junko Beat's sound draws from the rich musical heritage of New Orleans while incorporating modern influences, resulting in a style that is both rooted and forward-thinking. The group has become known for energetic live performances and a willingness to push the boundaries of traditional funk and jam genres.
