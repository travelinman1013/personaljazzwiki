## Biography
Kenneth Brian, born in Virginia and raised in Gainesville, Florida, with formative years in Alabama, has carved a niche in the music scene with his Southern Rock and Americana style. His musical journey began with his first album release in 2002, marking the start of a career that would span multiple genres, including Southern Rock, Alt Country, and Blues Rock. Over the years, Kenneth Brian has toured with notable artists such as Blackberry Smoke, Lucinda Williams, ZZ Top, and Lynyrd Skynyrd, showcasing his versatility and talent. His latest album, 'Keys to the Kingdom,' released in 2023, was recorded at Rancho De La Luna Studio in Joshua Tree, California, and features a blend of Southern Rock with Mojave Desert influences. Another notable album, 'With Lions,' released in 2022, was recorded in Hollywood and features a mix of rock, country, and punk elements. Kenneth Brian's music is characterized by soulful vocals and incendiary guitar work, earning him a loyal fan base and respect from fellow musicians. His style is deeply rooted in American Rock 'n' Roll, Blues, and Alternative Country, reflecting his diverse musical influences and experiences living in Southern California. With a career spanning over two decades, Kenneth Brian continues to captivate audiences with his unique sound and energetic performances. His band members include Chris Cano on drums and Brandon Owens on bass, among others, contributing to the dynamic sound of the Kenneth Brian Band. The band's ability to blend different genres has made them a staple in the Southern Rock scene, with their music often compared to artists like Drive-By Truckers and Lynyrd Skynyrd. Kenneth Brian's musical journey is marked by his ability to evolve and adapt, while maintaining a strong connection to his Southern roots.
