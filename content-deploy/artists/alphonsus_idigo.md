## Biography
Alphonsus Idigo is a Nigerian musician known for his work in digi-roots and cosmic boogie music during the 1980s. He released his album 'Search' in 1987 on the Idigo label (catalog number IDR 001), which showcased his distinctive blend of Nigerian musical traditions with electronic and experimental production techniques. The album, later reissued by the Italian independent label Dig This Way Records, has been recognized for its innovative approach to underground Nigerian music and its influence on contemporary world music collectors and researchers.
