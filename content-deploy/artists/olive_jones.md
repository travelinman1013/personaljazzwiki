## Biography
Olive Jones is a London-based singer-songwriter and guitarist whose music draws on soul, jazz, blues, and alt-folk traditions. Growing up, she was deeply influenced by the records of jazz greats and soul icons, which shaped her appreciation for the expressive power of the human voice. Her early years were spent singing along to these records, nurturing a vocal style that is both captivating and emotionally resonant[1][2][3].

Jones’s career has been marked by a commitment to exploring the nuances of human experience through her songwriting, with themes of love, loss, and communication at the core of her work. Her songs are characterized by groove-led arrangements, honey-drenched vocals, and lush harmonies, creating a sound that is both contemporary and timeless. She has collaborated with Leeds soul outfit Gotts Street Park, featuring as the lead vocalist on their track 'Tell Me Why,' which received national radio support and was championed by Elton John on his Rocket Man show[2][4].

Olive Jones’s live performances have further established her reputation, supporting artists such as Jalen Ngonda and Nitin Sawhney on UK and European tours. Her recent releases, including 'All In My Head' and 'End of Time,' showcase her wide vocal range and songwriting proficiency, blending classic R&B and soul with modern production techniques. As she prepares for her debut album and headline tour in 2026, Jones continues to carve a distinctive path in the contemporary R&B and soul music scenes[4][7].
