## Biography
Kenny Ellis is a Scottish jazz bassist who has established himself as a prominent figure in the UK jazz scene. He is best known as a member of BK3, a acclaimed jazz trio comprising pianist Brian Kellock, bassist Kenny Ellis, and drummer John Rae, which has been recognized as representing some of Scotland's finest jazz players. Ellis has contributed significantly to the Scottish jazz community through both his performances and his participation in archival documentation of the genre.
