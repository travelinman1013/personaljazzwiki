## Biography
Brad Walker is a prominent jazz saxophonist based in New Orleans. He holds a Bachelor of Music degree in classical saxophone performance and a Master of Music degree in jazz studies from Louisiana State University. Walker is known for his energetic and tasteful solos, which have been praised by publications like Rolling Stone and NPR. He has performed with numerous notable artists, including Sturgill Simpson, Bernard Purdie, and Marcia Ball. Walker has led horn sections for several Grammy-winning artists and has released five full-length albums under his own name. His debut album, 'Quintet', received a 4-star review in DownBeat magazine. Walker's career spans over a decade in New Orleans, where he is highly active in the music scene.
