## Biography
Erika Torres is a dynamic vocalist and baritone saxophonist based in New Orleans, celebrated for her vibrant blend of pop, R&B, and jazz influences. Originally from a small town in Northeastern Pennsylvania, Torres relocated to New Orleans in 2016 to immerse herself in the city’s storied music scene and pursue advanced studies in saxophone performance. She holds a master’s degree in music and has built a multifaceted career as both a performer and educator, regularly gigging at local venues while teaching aspiring musicians in the community.

Torres’s musical style is characterized by energetic rhythms, soulful vocals, and a deep sense of humanity, as showcased in her debut single 'Making Money.' Her artistry draws from the rich cultural tapestry of New Orleans, blending contemporary pop sensibilities with the improvisational spirit of jazz. Through her performances and recordings, Torres has established herself as a storyteller and visionary, using music as a means of personal expression and connection with her audience.

As her career continues to evolve, Torres has gained recognition both on stage and online, with her work resonating among listeners who appreciate authenticity and emotional depth. Her journey reflects resilience and creativity, and she remains committed to inspiring others through her music and teaching.
