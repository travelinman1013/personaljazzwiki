## Biography
India.Arie, born India Arie Simpson on October 3, 1975, in Denver, Colorado, is a celebrated neo soul and R&B singer-songwriter known for her introspective lyrics and acoustic-driven sound. Raised in a musically inclined family—her mother, Joyce, was a former singer and later her stylist, and her father, Ralph Simpson, was a professional basketball player—India.Arie began playing guitar at age 13. She attended Savannah College of Art and Design, where her growing interest in guitar led her to songwriting and performing, ultimately shaping her unique musical voice.

Her breakthrough came with the 2001 release of 'Acoustic Soul,' an album that blended soul, folk, and hip-hop influences and featured the empowering single 'Video.' The album garnered seven Grammy nominations and quickly went gold, establishing her as a leading figure in the neo soul movement. India.Arie followed up with 'Voyage to India' (2002), which debuted at number one on the Billboard R&B chart and won two Grammy Awards, and continued her success with albums like 'Testimony: Vol. 1, Life & Relationship' (2006) and 'Songversation' (2013). Over her career, she has sold over 10 million records worldwide and won four Grammy Awards from more than 20 nominations.

India.Arie's music is renowned for its messages of self-love, healing, and empowerment, drawing from a range of influences including Stevie Wonder and James Taylor. Her legacy is marked by her commitment to authenticity and positivity, making her a respected voice in contemporary R&B and soul.

## Fun Facts
- India.Arie's first name was chosen in honor of Mahatma Gandhi, as she shares his birthday (October 3).
- Her mother, Joyce Simpson, was a singer and later became India.Arie's stylist.
- She co-founded the Atlanta-based music collective Groovement/EarthShare before her mainstream success.
- India.Arie wrote and performed the song 'Just 4 2day' specifically for the premiere of The Tyra Banks Show.
