## Biography
Orchestra Superstring is a Los Angeles-based contemporary instrumental jazz group formed by veteran musicians, specializing in Latin jazz with influences from Afro-Cuban rhythms and bordering on exotica styles.[5][6][7] Emerging in the early 2000s or later, the band draws inspiration from mid-20th-century exotica pioneers while incorporating modern jazz elements, creating an exciting sound that blends Latin percussion, orchestral arrangements, and lounge-like atmospheres.[5][6] Their music evokes the exotic, relaxed vibes of tiki culture and global rhythms but updated for contemporary audiences, as evidenced by their vinyl debut album *Kalopia* released in 2012.[7]
