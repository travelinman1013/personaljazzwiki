## Biography
Carbon Poppies is a trio from Louisiana that emerged in the indie pop music scene during the 2010s. The group, formed by Allison Bohl and other members, developed a distinctive sound rooted in sixties-influenced pop sensibilities with a modern indie aesthetic. Their musical approach blends luminous pop arrangements with sharp, precious melodic sensibilities that harken back to classic pop songwriting traditions while maintaining contemporary production values.
