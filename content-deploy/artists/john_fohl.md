## Fun Facts
- Fohl has connections to the iconic film 'Animal House,' which he discussed in interviews about his early New Orleans musical beginnings
- He served as lead guitarist for the Cherry Poppin' Daddies from 1990-1992, a period when the band was developing their signature swing and ska sound
- Fohl is known as 'the laconic guitarist' for his storytelling ability, sharing anecdotes about working musicians and the New Orleans music scene
- His musical philosophy emphasizes minimalism and essence, deliberately stripping away unnecessary elements to focus on the fundamental 'teeth and bones' of his compositions
