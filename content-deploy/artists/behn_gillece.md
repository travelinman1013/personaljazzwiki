## Biography
Behn Gillece is a New Jersey-born vibraphonist and composer who has established himself as a prominent figure in contemporary jazz over the past decade. After honing his craft through numerous performance opportunities in his home state and Philadelphia, Gillece made the transition to the New York jazz scene, where he built a reputation as both a virtuosic instrumentalist and prolific composer. He holds a master's degree from SUNY Purchase and has been recognized with the 2018 Downbeat Rising Star Vibraphonist award, cementing his status as an emerging talent in jazz.

Gillece's musical approach is characterized by rhythmic drive and harmonic depth, with his compositions exploring themes of transition, momentum, and renewal. Working with top-tier ensembles, he has become a mainstay on the Posi-Tone Records label, where he has co-led projects and collaborated with notable saxophonists. His work demonstrates equal mastery of both performance and composition, showcasing his ability to navigate complex harmonic landscapes while maintaining accessibility and emotional resonance in his music.
