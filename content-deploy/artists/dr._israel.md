## Biography
Dr. Israel is a Brooklyn-based dub artist and revolutionary musician who emerged from New York City's experimental music scene.[2][3] His artistic practice is rooted in dub music, a genre originally developed in 1960s Jamaican reggae culture, which he uses as a foundational starting point for his own sonic explorations.[3][5][6] Dr. Israel's musical influences extend beyond dub to encompass jungle and drum'n'bass aesthetics, creating a hybrid approach that reflects the diverse sonic landscape of contemporary electronic and reggae-influenced music.[7] He has established himself as an active touring and recording artist, with releases on respected independent labels including ROIR Records, Wordsound, and MOD Tech, and has participated in various collaborations and remixes with established artists in his field.[2]
