## Biography
Beto Lopes is a Brazilian singer-songwriter and guitarist deeply rooted in the warm, introspective sounds of Música Popular Brasileira (MPB). His music blends nostalgic bossa nova textures with contemporary indie sensibilities, showcasing his melodic craftsmanship and storytelling ability. Lopes' recent releases, such as *Brasil* and *Variações do Tempo*, reflect his knack for weaving personal narratives with broader reflections on cultural identity and life's fleeting moments. He also pays tribute to Brazilian music legends through projects like *Nossas Mãos*, honoring Toninho Horta and the iconic Clube da Esquina collective, cementing his role as both a modern troubadour and a custodian of Brazil's rich musical legacy. Lopes' career development highlights his collaborations with artists like Duda Beat, further solidifying his position in the Brazilian music scene. His musical style is characterized by the use of acoustic guitar, vocals, and occasional trumpet, reflecting his versatility across Latin, Folk, and World genres.
