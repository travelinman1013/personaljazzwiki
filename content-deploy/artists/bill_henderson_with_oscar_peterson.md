## Biography
Bill Henderson was a jazz vocalist known for his work in genres including jazz, cool jazz, jazz ballads, bebop, and hard bop. He gained recognition through his 1963 collaboration with pianist Oscar Peterson's trio, resulting in the album 'Bill Henderson with the Oscar Peterson Trio,' recorded in February 1963 and featuring 16 tracks.[2][3] This Verve Records release showcased Henderson's vocal style alongside Peterson's acclaimed piano trio, highlighting his smooth delivery on standards like 'Wild Is Love' and 'You Are My Sunshine.'[4][5]
