## Biography
Josh Paxton is a New Orleans-based pianist renowned for his mastery of the city's deep piano traditions combined with modern jazz sensibilities. He studied piano with Ellis Marsalis, one of New Orleans' most influential jazz pianists, and developed a distinctive musical style that blends classical technique, contemporary jazz innovations, and funky rhythmic drive characteristic of the New Orleans sound. Throughout his career, Paxton has established himself as a versatile performer and collaborator, touring with a diverse range of artists spanning multiple genres and maintaining an active presence in the New Orleans music scene. He has become particularly recognized as an expert on James Booker, the legendary New Orleans pianist, and serves as a staple performer at WWOZ Piano Night, a celebrated venue for piano music in the city.
