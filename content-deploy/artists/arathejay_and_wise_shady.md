## Biography
Samuel Ofei Ankrah Badu, known professionally as AratheJay, was born on December 21st in Sekondi-Takoradi, Ghana, originally from the Eastern region. He spent a significant part of his early life in Kenya before currently residing in Tema, Ghana. A rising sensation in the Ghanaian music scene, AratheJay blends spirituality and positivity into his music, defining his sound under the umbrella of spiritual genres while incorporating elements of Afrobeats.[1][3][4]

AratheJay gained rapid recognition with his popular single 'Sankofa,' featuring a remix collaboration with Ghanaian superstar King Promise, and other tracks like 'Jesus Christ.' He studied human resources at university, emphasizing the importance of human capital and relations, which informs his focus on uplifting messages in his music. In interviews, he discusses his journey in the music industry, passion for Afrobeats, challenges faced, and aspirations to collaborate globally as a 'global citizen.'[1][2][6]

His career highlights include projects like 'The Capsule' with upcoming features from artists such as Bella, Black Sharie, and Grenade, alongside plans for more African collaborations. AratheJay aims to spread his message worldwide through diverse partnerships, addressing economic struggles in Ghana and Nigeria while prioritizing spiritual themes.[2]
